About Me

Hi there, I'm Mathias, and I'm deeply passionate about climbing. With 8 years of climbing experience under my belt, I've immersed myself in the world of vertical adventures, continually seeking new challenges and ways to improve as a climber.

Coaching and Specialisation

One of my greatest joys is sharing my climbing knowledge and expertise with others. I specialize in coaching and training children, fostering their love for climbing while teaching them the essential skills needed to excel in this demanding sport. Witnessing young climbers develop their strength, agility, and confidence.

Qualifications and Training

  • Climbing Wall Instructor (CWI)
  • Climbing Wall Development Instructor (CWDI)
  • Foundation Coach Trained
  • Development Coach Trained
  • Outdoor First Aid Level 3

British Mountaineering Training:

  • Fundementals 1, 2 and 3
  • Physical Training 1
  • Coaching For Kids
  • Setting For Coaches

Performance Squad Coach

I am proud to have been selected as a coach for the performance squad at The XC. Watching young people hone thier skills and push their limits is a privilege. In the Youth Climbing Series (YCS), some of my students have even stood on the podium, a testesment to their dedication and hard work.

Coaching Adults

While my specialization lies in coaching children, I'm also well-versed in teaching adults. Climbing is a sport for all ages, and I'm committed to helping climbers of all backgrounds and experience levels reach their full potential. Whether you're a beginner looking to conquer your first indoor route or an experienced climber aiming for new heights, I'm here to support your journey.

Constant Improvement

I firmly believe that climbing is a never-ending journey of self-discovery and growth. I'm dedicated to my own continual improvement and always striving to push my limits, both mentally and physically. From conquering challenging routes to refining my climbing technique, I'm up for every challenge that climbing throws my way.

Connect with Me

If you share my passion for climbing or want to explore this exhilarating sport, let's connect! Whether you're a parent looking for a climbing coach for your child or an adult seeking guidance on your climbing journey, I'm here to help.
